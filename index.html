About Me

I am an undergraduate student at Seoul National University, affiliated with the Autonomous Robot Intelligence Laboratory (ARIL).

My research interests focus on long-horizon task planning under uncertainty, where robots must reason over extended time horizons with incomplete or noisy information.

I am particularly interested in vision-language guided robotic task execution, aiming to bridge high-level semantic instructions and low-level embodied actions in real-world robotic systems.
